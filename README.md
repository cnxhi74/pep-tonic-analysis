## The Science Behind the Formula: Two Ingredients Worth Knowing

Most greens powders are glorified vegetable juice. Pep Tonic is trying to be something different, and two compounds explain why.

### Puremidine® — The Autophagy Trigger

Spermidine (marketed here as Puremidine®) is a naturally occurring polyamine compound. As you age, your body's levels of spermidine decline — and with them, the efficiency of autophagy. Autophagy is essentially your cells' housekeeping process: it identifies damaged, sluggish "zombie cells" and clears them out so newer, healthier cells can take their place.

When autophagy slows down, those zombie cells stick around. They drain energy, slow recovery, and accumulate in ways linked to accelerated aging. Puremidine® is included specifically to support this process. Each serving contains 1mg of spermidine trihydrochloride.

### MitoPrime® — The Longevity Vitamin

L-ergothioneine, sold as MitoPrime®, is a rare amino acid sometimes called the "longevity vitamin" by researchers studying cellular aging. It's a potent antioxidant that concentrates in mitochondria — the energy-producing parts of your cells — and helps shield them from oxidative stress. Each serving contains 5mg.

Why does mitochondrial health matter? Because when mitochondria are damaged or decline in function, you feel it: lower energy, slower recovery, brain fog. MitoPrime® works at that root level.
